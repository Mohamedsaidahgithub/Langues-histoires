Sidaa ayeey awoowyaasheeni hore u seexan jireen
Beri baa bare cashar u dhigaya fasal dugsigahoose ah ayaa intii ay ardaydu qorayeen casharkii uu sabburadda ku qoray ayaa waxa uu ku seexday miiskakorkiisa. Si kedis ah ayaa waxa soo galay fasalkii maamulihii dugsiga, inta barahii hurdadii ka salalay ayaa uu si degdeg ah u yiri " sidaa ayey awoowyaasheenii hore iyaga oo daalan u seexan jireen ."
MURTI IYO MALE'AWAAL:
Haddii ay gacantaadu ka gaabiso abaal marinta, carrabkaagu ha gaaro.
